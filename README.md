### 1. Total Price Varied by Category, Channel, Time, and Brand
Displays total sales amount across different brands and categories over time and through various sales channels.

### 2. Revenue by Category and Sub-Category
Treemap visualization categorizing revenue distribution:
- Major categories: **Electronics, Appliances, Computers**
- Subcategories: smartphones, TVs, refrigerators, notebooks, etc.
- Allows comparison of revenue contribution across product hierarchies.

### 3. Revenue by Brand
Bar chart displaying total revenue per brand:
- Quickly highlights **top-performing brands** (e.g., Apple, Samsung, Xiaomi).
- Enables tracking of long-tail brands with smaller revenue shares.

### 4. Special Promotion
Line chart comparing **Sum of Price** and **Revenue** over time (monthly basis).
- Helps assess the impact of promotions and discounts.
- Reveals trends in revenue vs. pricing strategies.

### 5. Traffic Varied by Day of Week and Channel
Dual-axis line and bar chart showing:
- **User traffic count** (App vs. Browser)
- Variation by **day of the week**
- Insights into when and where customers engage most.
